Le registre

Le Neo-Noir est le registre du contre-jour dur et de l'ombre portée. Le langage visuel du polar américain des années 80-2000 — Mann, De Palma, Fincher avant Fincher.

Néons sur asphalte mouillé. Silhouettes dans l'embrasure. Fumée qui diffuse la lumière. Contraste élevé, palette désaturée avec des touches de couleur isolée.

Ce que ce volume ne fait pas : de l'esthétique gratuite. Chaque choix lumineux a une intention narrative — l'ombre cache, la lumière accuse.

Aperçu des prompts

CORPS À CORPS — Prompt 01

Two fighters in warehouse, industrial lighting from above, concrete floor, one figure airborne mid-kick, the other in defensive stance, 35mm grain heavy, wide shot keeping both bodies in frame, 1990s Hong Kong action grammar in American setting, movement frozen at peak. Analog imperfection is intentional.

CORPS À CORPS — Prompt 02

Urban chase, pursuer and pursued visible simultaneously in wide frame, alley or market, practical daylight, motion blur on legs but faces sharp, 1980s action grammar, camera at ground level slightly, the chase readable without cuts. Analog imperfection is intentional.

EXPLOSIONS ET FEU — Prompt 03

Car explosion mid-frame, hero walking away in foreground, back to camera, fireball behind not yet reaching them, 1980s action poster grammar, practical fire as only light source, warm orange against cool blue sky, the hero larger than the destruction. Analog imperfection is intentional.

ARMES ET FUSILLADES — Prompt 04 — Accès Premium

Two positions of cover in same frame, muzzle flash visible from one, night, practical muzzle light as only illumination, 1990s action grammar, both combatants readable in composition, no fill light anywhere. Analog imperfection is intentional.
